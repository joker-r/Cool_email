Teknothon is the annual techno-cultural festival of Jamia Millia Islamia, New Delhi which promotes liberty, energy, enthusiasm and provides students with the opportunity to explore and rediscover themselves. Teknothon fosters creativity and imagination to stimulate the professional superiority and proficiency. By providing a platform to showcase student art forms, Teknothon creates a buzz of activity in the final weeks of the term, driving students to reach their full artistic potential in an engaging, accessible environment. Each year, faithful alumni, students, faculty, family, friends and the surrounding community participate in the premier festival celebrations where the Jamia Millia Islamia continues to remain a staple in their minds and hearts. 

Coupled with the legacy of fellowship and pride, the Teknothon celebration is one of the largest collegiate festival celebrations hence one of the most eagerly anticipated events in the University calendar. The success of the festival celebration, year after year, is the likely emanation of the collective efforts of students, alumni, faculty, and members of the community.
